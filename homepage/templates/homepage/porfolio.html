I am a graduate of Hope College with a degree in Computer Science and minor in Physics. I have loved science from childhood and want to apply my computer skills during a summer internship.

Last summer I worked at a medical device company in greater Chicago. I developed Labview software that automated the testing of medical pumps. I enjoyed the job very much but my parents moved to Portland, Oregon so I am looking for something that would allow me to live at home.

My interest in programming started in elementary school when I took a class on how to write software to create computer games. I also have a keen interest in robotics that was sparked by completing a LEGO mindstorms class.

I have done some cellular phone testing during high school. I tried to find areas where automated testing would catch failures. I operated the testing and recorded the failure rates.

I am creative and enjoy doing home projects. I have created a Halloween costume with wearable LED glasses. I enjoyed all aspects, from soldering to working out the circuit design.

I am a detailed oriented problem solver and good team player. I get a kick out of solving technical problems. I enjoy digging into the core of technology and using that knowledge to solve problems and come up with new applications.
